## The Challenge

Raw data is meaningless without context. We wanted to create a tool that transforms spreadsheets into stories.

## The Process

We studied how cartographers, artists, and data scientists think about visual representation. The intersection was stunning.

## The Solution

Cartograph uses WebGL-powered 3D visualizations that respond to your data in real-time, turning numbers into navigable landscapes.

## Impact

- **50K+** datasets visualized
- Featured in 3 design publications
- **98%** user satisfaction score
